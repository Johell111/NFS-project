Creche Department

A safe, loving and stimulating home away from home — 6 Months to 2 Years.

A Legacy of Care — 6 Months to 2 Years

At NOW Foundation, our Creche is designed to be a "home away from home." We understand that these formative months are critical for emotional security and brain development. Our environment is climate-controlled, sanitized hourly, and monitored by HD surveillance to ensure absolute peace of mind for parents.

  • Individualized Care: 1:3 caregiver-to-child ratio ensuring personalized attention to every feeding, nap and sleep schedule.
  • Sensory Development: Specialized "Soft-Play" zones designed to stimulate tactile, visual and auditory senses safely.
  • Health & Hygiene: Daily health checks by on-site nursing staff and a strictly organic-led nutritional support plan.
  • Early Bonding: Activities focused on emotional intelligence, social cues and secure attachment theory.
  • Sleep & Rest: Individual cots in dedicated quiet rooms with monitored sleep schedules and 24/7 CCTV oversight.
  • Parent Updates: Daily digital reports — meals, naps, mood and activities — delivered directly to parents.
  • Organic Nutrition: All meals freshly prepared on-site by trained nutritionists following age-appropriate dietary guidelines.

Nursery School

Building foundations that last a lifetime through play, discovery and guided learning.

Building the Foundation — Ages 2 to 5

Our Nursery program utilizes a blended curriculum of Montessori and the British Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS). We transition children from play-based discovery to structured cognitive learning, fostering a love for inquiry that lasts a lifetime.

  • Phonetic Mastery: Introduction to the Jolly Phonics system to build early reading and pronunciation excellence.
  • Logical Reasoning: Hands-on numeracy activities using tactile tools to grasp concepts of quantity and logic.
  • Creative Arts: Dedicated studio time for music, painting and drama to encourage self-expression and confidence.
  • Social Grace: Weekly Etiquette Classes — table manners, public speaking and collaborative teamwork.
  • Outdoor Learning: Structured outdoor play sessions integrated with nature-based discovery activities.
  • Emotional Intelligence: Circle time and guided storytelling to develop empathy and emotional self-regulation.
  • Bilingual Exposure: Basic French vocabulary introduced through songs, games and storytelling from Nursery 2.

Primary School

Where academic excellence meets modern leadership — preparing global citizens of tomorrow.

The Leadership Track — Ages 5 to 10

The Primary School at NOW Foundation is where academic excellence meets modern leadership. Our rigorous curriculum prepares students for the challenges of global secondary education, emphasizing critical thinking over rote memorization.

Advanced Academics

  • Mathematics: Singapore Math — mastery and problem-solving focused.
  • Science: Lab-based Biology, Physics and Earth Sciences.
  • English Literacy: Analytical reading and creative writing workshops.
  • Social Studies: Civics, geography and cultural awareness from Primary 1.
  • Religious Studies: Values-based moral education.

Modern Skills

  • ICT & Robotics: Weekly coding classes and engineering principles.
  • Leadership: Public speaking, debate and community service projects.
  • Global Languages: French and Mandarin foundations.
  • Physical Education: Structured sports, teamwork and fitness programs.
  • Life Skills: Time management and financial literacy basics.
